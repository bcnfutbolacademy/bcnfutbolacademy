About David Ray-Campoy

David Ray-Campoy, born in 2000 in the European city of Barcelona, Spain, started playing fútbol at 8 years old. Through his unique methodology focusing on Balance, Creativity, and Navigation, he guides players to reach their full potential on and off the field.

With 7+ years of coaching experience and a UEFA C coaching license (obtained in 2017), David designs each training session to challenge players technically, tactically, physically, and mentally while encouraging creative decision-making and problem-solving skills.
